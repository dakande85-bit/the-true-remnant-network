# Remnant Platform Instructions

Build The True Remnant Network as a premium Christian interview, conversation, and end-times discernment ministry.

## Product Identity

The True Remnant Network interviews people of faith and brings believers together to discuss the difficult questions facing the Body of Christ. The ministry seeks biblical truth, understands context, strengthens unity, and helps believers become of one mind in Christ.

Brand message: One Body. One Mind. One Truth in Christ.

Key verse: Philippians 2:2.

Supporting verse: 1 Corinthians 1:10.

## Beliefs

- Jesus Christ is the Son of God and the only way to the Father.
- The true Gospel must remain central.
- Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
- Scripture is the final authority.
- Context matters when discussing doctrine.
- The Body of Christ needs unity around truth, not shallow agreement.
- We are living in the last days.
- Scripture warns of deception, tribulation, the rise of the Antichrist, and the return of Jesus Christ.
- End-times teaching should be serious, biblical, and without fear-mongering.

## Design Direction

- Premium Christian media ministry
- Editorial and cinematic
- Clean, modern, trustworthy
- Not old-fashioned church design
- Strong typography
- Warm neutral colours: cream, black, off-white, and gold accent
- Mobile-first

## MVP Rules

- Do not add in-site payments.
- Use external links for PayPal donations, YouTube, websites, books, music, and events.
- Use mock/example data until Supabase is configured.
- Keep data access isolated so Supabase can replace the mock provider cleanly.
- Keep the MVP simple and production-ready.

## Copy Rules

- Do not make the site feel like an open public directory.
- Do not lead with submit-organisation or public validation-status language.
- Keep resources secondary to interviews, conversations, doctrine, prophecy, prayer, and unity in Christ.
- Speak about the last days seriously and biblically without fear-mongering, conspiracy-led messaging, or sensationalism.
- Keep the tone biblical, serious, Christ-centred, end-times aware, and rooted in unity around truth.
